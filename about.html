About Doemageddon

Ethical hunting. Purpose-driven giving. Community first.

Who We Are

Doemageddon is a nonprofit organization formed by hunters with a heart for helping. We believe in the power of the outdoors to not only provide sustenance, but also build stronger, more compassionate communities.

What We Do

Our annual hunt gathers ethical, licensed hunters across Michigan to responsibly harvest deer. The processed venison is donated directly to food banks, shelters, and families in need — ensuring nothing goes to waste and everything goes to good use.

Our Mission

To unite hunters across Michigan in service to their communities by ethically harvesting deer and donating the meat to those in need.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we've provided thousands of pounds of venison to families across the state — feeding the hungry, reducing deer overpopulation, and strengthening community ties in the process.
