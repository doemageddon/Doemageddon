Event Rules

  1. All participants must be registered prior to the start of the hunt.
  2. Only antlerless deer (does) are eligible for submission.
  3. All deer must be harvested legally and ethically according to Michigan DNR regulations.
  4. Each harvested deer must be dropped off at an approved donation station within 12 hours.
  5. All meat donations will be inspected and processed for local shelters and food banks.

  7. No baiting, spotlighting, or trespassing is allowed. Violators will be disqualified.
  8. Final tally and awards will be announced at the closing ceremony.
