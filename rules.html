Event Rules

All hunters are expected to follow these guidelines to ensure a safe, ethical, and meaningful hunt.

  • All participants must be registered by November 23rd at 11:59 PM.
  • Only antlerless deer (does) are eligible for submission.
  • All deer must be harvested legally and ethically according to Michigan DNR regulations.
  • All deer must be harvested on the day of the event. (December 6th, 2025)
  • Each registered hunter can bring one (1) doe to Apex Custom Butchering for weigh in and donation.
  • Each harvested deer must be brought to Apex Custom Butchering for weigh in by 6:30 PM.
  • All deer meat will be donated to local food banks after the event.
  • Participants must wear hunter’s orange at all times according to the MDNR.
  • No baiting, spotlighting, or trespassing is allowed. Violators will be disqualified.
  • Winner will be determined by the heaviest DRESSED doe
  • If there is a tie, tiebreaker will be whichever deer was weighed first.
  • Final tally and awards will be announced at the dinner.
