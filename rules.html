Event Rules

All hunters are expected to follow these guidelines to ensure a safe, ethical, and meaningful hunt.

  • All participants must be registered prior to the start of the hunt.
  • Only antlerless deer (does) are eligible for submission.
  • All deer must be harvested legally and ethically according to Michigan DNR regulations.
  • Each harvested deer must be dropped off at an approved donation station within 12 hours.
  • All meat donations will be inspected and processed for local shelters and food banks.

  • No baiting, spotlighting, or trespassing is allowed. Violators will be disqualified.
  • Final tally and awards will be announced at the closing ceremony.
